Often, You have the need to share a written demonstration of the information you wish to pass, but your peer(s) live far away and video calls are not sufficient to whollistially convey the message; Collab helps you achieve this.

with Collab, you can have a video conference with a shared board where every peer has access to the board and can make annotations on it.

The idea for this project was born when its co-developers realized they needed a board to share design ideas visually and live while communicating.

There are solutions already for this, but some have caps on communication time while others require payment for group communications. We decided to build a simple version to solve our problem as our final project at ALX software enginnering course.
