Often you want to draw out your ideas
during conversation with a peer,
but distance is a barrier.

Collab helps you achieve that by
providing a platform for video
conferencing and a shared canvas for
sharing your ideas.

Collab uses PeerJs for the peer to peer
communication.

Collab was developed as the final
project for its co-developers in the ALX
software engineering program.
